# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This draft describes the current prototype behavior for CreditCrest AI and the related CrestLend sandbox app. It is not a final production privacy policy.

## Current Product Mode

CreditCrest AI is an educational prototype. CrestLend is a demo/sandbox lending journey app. Both use synthetic or demo data for product exploration, education, and testing.

## Data Currently Used

- Synthetic demo profiles and scenarios
- Optional local-only educational inputs, such as rounded financial snapshot or sandbox borrower values
- Local UI state, such as checklist progress or demo profile preferences

Where local storage applies, data is stored only on the user device/browser. It is not sent to a backend service by the current app builds.

## Data Not Collected

The current apps do not collect:

- Social Security numbers
- Full dates of birth
- Bank credentials
- Credit bureau credentials
- Real identity documents
- Real uploaded files
- Account numbers
- Real loan applications

## Lending And Credit Boundaries

The current apps do not:

- Approve or deny real users
- Match users with real lenders
- Show real lender offers
- Submit data to banks, bureaus, lenders, or document providers
- Calculate official credit scores
- Guarantee credit, lending, or approval outcomes

## Data Sharing And Sale

The current apps do not sell user data. The current apps do not share local demo/sandbox inputs with lenders, banks, credit bureaus, advertisers, or data brokers.

## Production Note

Before production activation, this policy would need legal review and updates for any backend services, analytics, partner integrations, identity verification, document upload, credit bureau soft pulls, open banking, payment processing, retention rules, user rights, and security controls.
